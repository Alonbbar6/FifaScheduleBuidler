Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 9, 2026

Introduction

FIFA Schedule Builder ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in our iOS application.

Information We Collect

Our app may collect the following types of information:

  • Schedule Preferences: Your favorite teams, saved matches, and personal schedule preferences stored locally on your device
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use the app to improve our services
  • Chat Interactions: Conversations with our AI chatbot to provide assistance (processed securely and not stored permanently)

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the app functionality
  • Personalize your experience
  • Improve and optimize the app
  • Respond to your support requests

Data Storage and Security

Most of your data is stored locally on your device. Any data transmitted to external services (such as AI features) is encrypted and processed securely. We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Third-Party Services

Our app may use third-party services for:

  • AI chatbot functionality (OpenAI API)
  • Analytics to understand app usage

These services have their own privacy policies governing the use of your information.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the data we store about you
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of data collection features

Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.
